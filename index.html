Cryptographic Email Verification

This demo represents a Relying Party (RP)—a website (like an e-commerce or SaaS site) verifying a user's email. Using the Email Verification Protocol (EVP), we verify the email address instantly and cryptographically with the Identity Provider (IdP), eliminating the need to send a traditional one-time passcode (OTP) or verification link.

Fill in your email. If your browser supports EVP, selecting your email from the autofill dropdown will automatically populate the hidden cryptographic token.
